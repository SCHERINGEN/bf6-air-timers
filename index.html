Voice Commands
TTS Callouts

How to use

Select a preset (map and mode) or add cards manually, kill stuff, set timers, never get backstabbed again.

Tips

  • Voice commands are optional, you can use the buttons if you prefer.
  • TTS will only call out when timers run out and to confirm when executing voice commands.

Voice Commands 🎤

Start Timers

  • Say "Timer" and the vehicle alias
  • "Timer Scout" will start the timer on a scout card

Activate locked Escalation Cards

  • Say "Activate" and the vehicle alias
  • "Activate Scout" will activate a locked scout card

Add Cards

  • Say "Add" and the vehicle alias
  • "Add Scout" will add a scout card

Remove Cards

  • Say "Remove" and the vehicle alias
  • "Remove Scout" will remove a scout card

Replace Cards

  • Say "Replace" and the vehicle alias of the card to replace, then the vehicle alias to replace with
  • "Replace Scout Attack" will replace a scout card with an attack card
